# LaunchDeck Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 3, 2026*

## Overview

LaunchDeck is an Xbox Game Bar widget that launches applications and URLs from a configurable grid. It operates entirely on your local device.

## Data Collection

LaunchDeck does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information. It does not use analytics, telemetry, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

## Local Data

LaunchDeck stores your configuration (application shortcuts and layout preferences) in a local JSON file on your device. This data never leaves your computer.

## Network Access

LaunchDeck does not make any network requests. The `internetClient` capability declared in the package is required by the Xbox Game Bar framework and is not used by LaunchDeck itself.

## Third-Party Services

LaunchDeck does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/Tien-Lam/LaunchDeck).
